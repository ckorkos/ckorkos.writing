Level Design & Landscaping

Vivec City Gatehouse

The Ordinators of Vivec are pledged, body and soul, to Vvardenfell's ruling Tribunal Temple. Their Order of the Watch guards Vivec from crime, heresy, and blight.

The Garland Gatehouse is the north-facing gate of Vivec, connecting to the larger world through river docks and silt striders. To help design the interior of this fortress, I provided landscaping recommendations and in-game texts that demonstrated the disciplined training, worship, and law practiced by the Order of the Watch.

Hlaalu Canalworks

The canalworks toil beneath each of Vivec's cantons, recycling water from the city's network of canals and aqueducts. They are managed through dangerous and difficult work, and are home to those with few other options. Beneath them are the Underworks - festering sewers untouched even by the vicious boot of the Ordinators.

The Hlaalu Canalworks sit beneath the canton of Great House Hlaalu. To help design the interior of these dimly lit hallways, I provided landscaping recommendations and in-game texts that evidenced the arduous life hidden beneath Vivec's gilded surface.
