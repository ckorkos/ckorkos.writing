Chuzei Bonemold Helm

Item Type

  • Helm - Medium Armor
  • Not Enchanted

Description

  • An ancestral helm of bone from an antique age. Its double slit view, designed to emulate Vvardenfell's unique fauna, provides increased visibility for warriors fighting at range.

    House Chuzei, furious at an alleged betrayal by the Tribunal, broke from House Indoril and followed Azura to the untamed Ashlands. Their final collapse is shrouded by time, but vestiges of their artistry remain.
