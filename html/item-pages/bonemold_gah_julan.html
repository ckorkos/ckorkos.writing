Gah-Julan Helm

Item Type

  • Helm - Medium Armor
  • Not Enchanted

Description

  • A practical yet distinctive helm of softened bone, given shape by shalk resin. Its thick cloth hood repels the ash that continually rains from the island's heart. To many in Vvardenfell, this is the true face of House Redoran.

    "A Telvanni speaks through magicka, mysticism, and things illusory. A Hlaalu speaks through commerce, politics, and things deceitful. A Redoran must speak through honor and duty, for these are our enduring foundations."
    - Archmaster Venim, Martial Virtues

Gah-Julan Cuirass

Item Type

  • Cuirass - Medium Armor
  • Not Enchanted

Description

  • Plates of bone from various species, sealed with resin to maximize collective strength. Their jagged layers evoke Red Mountain's treacherous peaks. This cuirass forgoes the Daedric runes adorning the armor of rival Great Houses, instead embodying a severe minimalism.

    "To rely on symbols is to shackle oneself to the unreal. Character must stem from one's honorable actions, both in victory and in defeat."
    - Archmaster Venim, Martial Virtues
×

Gah-Julan Gauntlets

Item Type

  • Gauntlets - Medium Armor
  • Not Enchanted

Description

  • Flexible netch-leather gloves guarded by an exoskeleton of layered bone. They are resilient enough to turn a blade, yet nimble enough to string a bow. The scarred shell suggests either a lifetime of battle, or a singular ferocity in the creatures from which they are derived.

    "One's hands must be employed in the duties of war and scripture. The first, for survival. The second, for immortality."
    - Archmaster Venim, Martial Virtues
×

Gah-Julan Boots

Item Type

  • Boots - Medium Armor
  • Not Enchanted

Description

  • Robust boots of molded bone and blight-moth wool. Though irritating, they insulate the wearer from the heat of Red Mountain and the jaws of nix-hounds. Redoran children are taught that in their ashen homeland, a crippled extremity is as sure a death sentence as a torn jugular.

    "One must follow the Fifth Walking Way. To march in the shadow of Lord Vivec, one requires a disciplined mind, a pious heart, and an unshakeable stride."
    - Archmaster Venim, Martial Virtues

Gah-Julan Pauldrons

Item Type

  • Pauldron - Medium Armor
  • Not Enchanted

Description

  • These pauldrons seal the cuirass from Vvardenfell's noxious air like a cocoon. As construction on the Ghostfence began to strain resources, the island's Dunmer abandoned their tradition of integrating ancestors' corpses into new armor. Nevertheless, some of these bones are unnervingly humanoid.

    "To shoulder the weight of their ancestors, one must embrace the belief that true immortality belongs not to an individual, but to a people."
    - Archmaster Venim, Martial Virtues

Gah-Julan Greaves

Item Type

  • Greaves - Medium Armor
  • Not Enchanted

Description

  • Often unappreciated by novices, these broad greaves will prevent a slow death by severed tendon. Survivors assert they live up to their namesake.

    "The first bonemold was forged from corpses in a besieged stronghold, and has since been of great benefit to us. In times of strife, one must be prepared to offer all they are to strengthen their people."
    - Archmaster Venim, Martial Virtues

Gah-Julan Tower Shield - Version 1

Item Type

  • Standard Shield - Medium Armor
  • Not Enchanted

Description

  • Popular among Redoran warriors for its balanced defense and maneuverability. To its bearer, the shield boasts the macabre sight of once-molten bone stretched over a blisterwood grip. To its enemy, the shield presents an omen of their future.

    "The honorable dead are blessings to us - be they friend or foe. The blasphemous dead are as cursed as the ground they stain."
    - Archmaster Venim, Martial Virtues

  • Not Enchanted
