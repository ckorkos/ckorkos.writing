Project Overview

Skywind is a full recreation of The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind, built using The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim’s Creation Engine. The fan-driven project will offer a modern experience following the plot of the original game, including updated combat and leveling, fully voiced characters, and new secrets to uncover. As a writer for Skywind, I’ve created descriptions for in-game items, dialogue for character encounters, and quest outlines. I’ve collaborated with fellow writers to brainstorm quest design and dialogue, as well as with designers and developers to ensure my writing matches the visual and environmental experience of its world.
