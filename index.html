About Me

Since childhood, I've loved fantasy and science fiction. Where fantasy was the call to adventure, science fiction was appropriately cosmic - crafting utopian dreams which we must strive to build, or twisted realities driven by our most sinister natures.

I studied Literature Writing and Human-Computer Interaction at University of California, San Diego, but I've continued my journey as a writer beyond education. My goal is to write for game development, which merges the disciplines of art, technology, music, drama, and narrative to bring life to these many worlds.
