Christopher Korkos

Game Writing & Narrative Design

Project: Skywind

Role: Writer

May 2022 - Present

Since I first delved into literature and entertainment, I've embraced the genres within speculative fiction. To me, fantasy was the childhood call to adventure that lives in us all. Science fiction, however, was appropriately cosmic - crafting utopian dreams which we must strive to build, or twisted realities driven by our most sinister natures.

I completed a Minor in Literature Writing at University of California, San Diego, but I've continued my journey as a writer beyond education. My goal is to write for the field of game design, which merges the disciplines of art, technology, music, drama, and narrative to bring life to these many worlds.
